Land & sea pollution

Computers, when people finished using them, are put in the dump. This would cause land pollution as the toxic chemicals in the computer will pollute the soil. This would overtime contaminate the plants around it and would effect the animals.

For sea pollution the exact same thing happens but this times it can kill the life within the sea due to the toxic chemicals spreading to the ocean and sea if computers are dumped into the river

air pollution

Computers add more to power plants. This is because more energy is needed to power all the computers in the world. This effects the air because tons of CO2 and other greenhouses gases are emmited into thr air. This would lead to global warming

Noise pollution

While over the years businesses have been investing money to reduce noise of computer components, they still create lots of noise. This is because PC components like the CPU generate alot of heat. This causes the need for fans. These fans can generate alot of noise in order to keep the computer cool. Hard drives would also cause alot of noise because as the platters than spin cause alot of noise and vibrations




E-waste

E-waste is any electrical device thats been discarded. This is due to people putting electrical devices into the bin instead of recycling or reusing them. This then ends up in landfill and causing dangerous amounts of land pollution.

    Here are some statistics of e-waste:

  • 20-50 million tonnes of e-waste is generated each year
  • the Uk disposes 1 million tonnes of e-waste each year
  • 45 tonnes of e-waste for every 100 tonenes are re-used. - thats only 45% of people who are recycling each year!

However the EU has been trying to counter-act this. A new law was introduced to oblige companies to accept smaller electronics for recycling
